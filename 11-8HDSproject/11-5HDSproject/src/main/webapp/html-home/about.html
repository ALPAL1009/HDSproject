About Us

CUSTOM KITCHEN REMODELING

Joe and Emily Herold are the proud owners of Home Design Solutions (HDS) remodeling company based in Phoenix, Arizona that specializes in upgrading bathrooms and kitchens. Since 2007 the goal has always been to serve our customers and provide an environment that empowers them to make the best choices for their project. We plan on growing and offering more services to our clients but overall, we guarantee you will be incredibly pleased with our work and could not be happier with the outcome. At present, we serve customers in 5 store locations across Arizona, New Mexico, and Nevada with almost 40 employees bringing high-quality kitchen and bathroom designs and build outs.
