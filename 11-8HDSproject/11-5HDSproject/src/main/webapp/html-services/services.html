Our Services


Current Fees:



Design Fees - $100 per hour; minimum of $500 per job (includes onsite visits).

Installation of Cabinets - $75 per hour; minimum of $750 per kitchen.

Re-facing Cabinets - $75 per hour; minimum of $750 per kitchen.

Plumbing Work - $85 per hour; minimum of $500 per kitchen.

Electrical Work - $85 per hour; minimum of $500 per kitchen.

Painting/Wallpaper Work - contracted to outside painting contractor (AZ Design). They charge a minimum of $500 to paint a kitchen and $40 per hour to hang wallpaper. The client meets with the sub-contractor for details.

General Construction work - moving walls, sheetrock, framing, windows, etc. Some of this work is contracted out if needed, but normal charges are $60.00 per hour for labor.

Deliveries - $50 minimum on 1st piece and then $25 for each extra piece. Large items will incur a $100 surcharge for delivery. Any installation required will be charged at the standard hourly rate of $85 per hour.
